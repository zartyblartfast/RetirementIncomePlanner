Methodology & Data Sources

Equity returns: 70% S&P 500 total return (price + dividends) + 30% UK share price return (price only, no dividends). UK component understates total return.

Bond returns: UK gilt yields (BoE Millennium), approximated as yield + 10yr duration × (−Δyield).

Inflation: UK CPI from BoE Millennium (to 2016) + ONS CPI (2017+).

Cash: Bank of England Bank Rate.

Method: Each pot’s return is weighted by its asset allocation. The projection is run for every viable historical period of the required length. Results show how the scenario would have performed across 100+ years of real market history.
